
# Liability Analysis: False Negatives in CKD AI

## 1. Medical Negligence (Tort)
- Duty: Reasonable standard of care
- Breach: Blind reliance on AI without validation
- Causation: FN → delayed diagnosis → progression
- Damages: Increased morbidity, dialysis risk

## 2. Black Box Defense
Opacity is not a defense where **explainability tools are available**. Courts trend toward requiring auditability.

## 3. Product vs Service Liability
- Developer: product defect (design, warning)
- Hospital: deployment & governance
- Doctor: clinical judgment

## 4. Standard of Care (2026)
AI is **assistive**. Failure to override or double-check is negligence in high-risk contexts.

## 5. Allocation
Primary (Doctor) → Secondary (Hospital) → Conditional (Developer)

## 6. Analogies
Radiology AI misses; AV liability frameworks.
