
# Malpractice in the Age of Algorithmic Diagnostics

## Abstract
We analyze liability from false negatives in CKD AI systems.

## Methods
Synthetic CKD dataset; LR, RF, XGB, NN; threshold tuning for recall; SHAP/LIME.

## Results
All models exhibit FNs; tuned thresholds reduce FN at FP cost.

## Legal Analysis
Explainability and governance reduce liability exposure.

## Conclusion
Recall-first design + human oversight is essential.
